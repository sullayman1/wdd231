LookUp English language institute

Site Name: Lookup English

Why this name?: "Lookup" gives off a vibe of aiming higher and improving yourself, which is perfect for an institute that helps people boost their English skills for online jobs or U.S. university programs.

Optional domain idea: lookupenglish.org (you could check if it's available)

Scenarios

How can I learn English to apply for online jobs?

How good does my english need to be to be successful at school?

Site Purpose:

The Lookup English website is here to help people learn English, specifically if they’re looking to get an online job or get into a U.S. online university. It’ll have all the info on courses, resources, and tips to meet English requirements for both jobs and university admissions.
